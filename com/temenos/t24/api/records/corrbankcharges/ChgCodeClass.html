        public TField getChgAmt()
        Returns the ChgAmt

        Field description: Specify the charge amount applicable for the currency. Incase the Charge amount is not entered then based on the charge type, amount is defaulted here. Incase the charge amount involves other than local currency then the amount is arrived by applying exchange rate for the currency market specified in Ccy.Market and stored in next field. Amount available here is directly mapped to CHARGE.AMT in FT application. Validation Rules: Optional field If not entered defaulted from FT.CHARGE.TYPE.
