      • getCalcDate

        public TField getCalcDate()
        Returns the CalcDate

        Field description: Mandatory field to capture the trailer fee calculation date. This field should be set in such a way that the final calculation date coincides with the payment generation date set at the issuer level arrangement. For example, Case 1: Consider the below values CALC.DATE 01 JAN 2008 CALC.FREQ 01 JAN 2008 M0101 PAYMENT.FREQ 01JUN2008M0601 In the above case, there will be six calculation before the payment generation and the final calculation date (01JUN2008) coincides with the payment date. So this setup is allowed. Case 2: Consider the below values CALC.DATE 01 JAN 2008 CALC.FREQ 01 JAN 2008 M0201 PAYMENT.FREQ 01 JUN 2008 M0601 In the above case, there will be three calculation viz., 01JAN, 01MAR, 01MAY and the next calculation date will be cycled to 01JUL. The calculation date (01 MAY 2008) before the payment generation date does not coincide with the PAYMENT.FREQ date and hence this setup is not allowed. Validation Rules: Date cannot be less than Today Standard date field.

        public TField getLastPayDate()
        Returns the LastPayDate

        Field description: Specifies the latest of the payment dates when SC.TRAIL.FEES.HOLDING record is marked as complete. Initially it will get defaulted to one calendar day prior to the START.PAY.DATE Validation Rules: This is a single value and Noinput field. For example, consider two payment cycle is crossed and two SC.TRAIL.FEES.HOLDING records (as on 01st Jan and 01st Feb) is generated on the payment date. If the second payment record generated on 01st Feb is marked as Complete, then LAST.PAY.DATE will be updated with 01st Feb. Even after subsequent payment completion of the first payment record generated on 01st Jan, this field will not be overridden with 01st Jan and will still retain the latest of the payment dates.
