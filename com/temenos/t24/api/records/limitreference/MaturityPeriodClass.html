        public TField getMatPeriodPerc()
        Returns the MatPeriodPerc

        Field description: This percentage will be used in deriving a final limit percentage. The resulting percentage amount of each transaction movement will be monitored against the limit amount. The percentages may be specified in a BAND or LEVEL basis (see LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL), and may be taken as a FIXED or PRORATA percentage (see PERC.CALC.BASIS). Associated with MATURITY.PERIOD. Note: Percentage definitions defined in fields LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL through to PERC.CALC.BASIS are ignored for updates from COMMITMENT contracts. Validation Rules: 1-11 Numeric characters (1-6 decimals) percentage must be less than 100%. (No change) Input is not allowed in this field for Fiduciary and Deposit Limit Reference records.

      • getMaturityPeriod

        public TField getMaturityPeriod()
        Returns the MaturityPeriod

        Field description: Indicates the remaining time to maturity for a transaction to which a percentage applies. The period defined here represents the remaining time to maturity for a contract. Together with MAT.PERIOD.PERC, this field allows the amount of a transaction used to monitor limimts to vary with the length of time left to maturity. The periods can be specified on a BAND or LEVEL basis, defined in LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL. Periods specified here are inclusive of this date. The amount of a transaction used to update the limit system will be recalculated each night in accordance with the specified period. Associated with MAT.PERIOD.PERC. Note: Percentage definitions defined in fields LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL through to PERC.CALC.BASIS are ignored for updates from COMMITMENT contracts. When using a BANDED or a PRORATA structure it is possible that the total transaction amount calculated ma exceed 100% of the transaction value. This can be avoided by using a PERCENTAGE.CAP of 100%. Example: The following percentage structure may be defined: Transactions maturing in up to 6 months are recorded at 5% Transactions maturing in up to 12 months are recorded at 6% Transactions maturing in up to 24 months are recorded at 7% Transactions maturing more than 24 months are recorded at 10% This is entered as:

        MATURITY.PERIOD6M12M24MR
        MAT.PERIOD.PERC5%6%7%10%
        The risk on a loan with a 36 month initial period would be calculcaled as: At input: LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL = LEVEL 10% of the value (36 months remaining falls in the rest period) LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL = BAND With 18 months remaining: LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL = LEVEL 7% of the value (18 months remaining falls in the 12 - 24 month band) LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL = BAND 5% of the value (the loan falls in the 0 - 6 month band) 6% of the value (the loan falls in the 6 - 12 month band) 7% of the value (the loan falls in the 12 - 24 month band) The percentage(s) extracted can be calculated on a PRORATA or FIXED basis (see PERC.CALC.BASIS for an example). Validation Rules: 'R' for Remaining or nnA where nnn - Number of A where: A = Y for years M for months W for weeks or D for days e.g. a 12 month period could ne input as 12M or 1Y. (No change) The numbers of days, weeks, months or years must be entered in a logical order i.e. in ascending sequence. When more than one period is defined a consistent unit must be used. R must be used as the final item.
