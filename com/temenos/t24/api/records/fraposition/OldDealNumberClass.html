        public TField getOldDealPL()
        Returns the OldDealPL

        Field description: The Profit or Loss on the deal resulting from the closure of a position would be updated to the field DEAL.P.L.

        This field is part of an association of fields (OLD.DEAL.NUMBER to OLD.DEAL.P&L.EQUIV) which contains all "TRADE" FRA deals with Trade date before Today.

        During the end of day process, the deals in these fields from DEAL.NUMBER to DEAL.P&L.EQUIV (containing all "TRADE" FRA deals of Today and Trade dated today) will be moved to the other association of fields OLD.DEAL.NUMBER to OLD.P&L.EQUIV which contain deals of previous Trade dates.

        Validation Rules: This field is updated by the system. 1 to 19 Numeric character (standard amount field)
