      • getPartAmt

        public TField getPartAmt()
        Returns the PartAmt

        Field description: Share of the participant in the loan expressed in deal currency. System would default the name share and amount in base currency once Deal.Currency and Amount fields are filled up. If Deal.Currency and Base.Ccy.Amt is input, share of participants in facility currency (in field Part.Amt.B.Ccy field) would be defaulted. The user may however, modify the share provided none of the participant's portion is overdrawn. Amount expressed in this field is in deal currency. During a Principal movement; The redistribution of the participant's share would be done at the ratio at which the Loan is currently held. This can however be changed by the User. In the event of such a change, for a Principal Increase the redistributed share for any participant cannot be less than what was already outstanding. Likewise for a Principal Decrease, the redistributed share cannot be greater than what was already outstanding. Validation Rules: 1-14 numeric character plus decimal point System defaulted amount. Modifiable by user.

        Returns:
        TField - The PartAmt
