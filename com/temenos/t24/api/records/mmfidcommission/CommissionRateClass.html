      • getAmountLimit

        public TField getAmountLimit()
        Returns the AmountLimit

        Field description: Specifies the maximum amount of principal to which the rate defined in the previous field is applicable. The system will apply the rate input at the previous field to all contracts of a value up to and including the amount in this field. Any contract over this amount will have its commission calculated at the next rate (if any) in the tier of multivalue fields up to and including the next maximum amount specified. For Example:- FIELD INPUT 3.1 Commission Rate 5.0% 4.1 Amount.Limit 50,000.00 () 3.2 Commission Rate 4.00% 4.2 Amount.Limit 100,000.00 () 3.3 Commission Rate 3.0% 4.3 Amount.Limit 250,000.00 () Assume three fiduciary deposits are loaded, each one for a 61 day period, but with principal amounts of 50,000.00, 100,000.00 and 250,000.00 respectively. Then:- a) on 1st contract fiduciary commission is 50,000.00 X 61 X 5% is 417.81 36,500 b) on 2nd contract fiduciary commission is 100,000.00 X 61 X 4% is 668.49 36,500.00 c) on 3rd contract fiduciary commission is 250,000.00 X 61 X 3% is 1,253.42 36,500.00 Note: The day basis used in the calculation is taken from the INTEREST.BASIS field on the contract. From the examples given above it will be seen that the system always calculates a flat commission on the face value of the contract. If the principal amount on the contract changes during the period over which commission is calculated, then a weighted average of the individual principal amount will be used. The associated multivalue fields 3.1 and 4.1 may be expanded up to a maximum of 999, but however many are used, the last amount field of the group must be the maximum amount allowed by the system for the appropriate currency. eg for a '2' decimal currency: 999,999,999,999.99 for a '0' decimal currency: 99,999,999,999,999 Validation Rules: Standard amount format. (Multivalue field associated with field 3) (Optional input ; default value is maximum amount permitted by system ie 14 x 9's). The fiduciary commission calculated by each tier must not be greater or equal to the maximum commission amount (field 2) except for the last tier. The amount limits must be in ascending order.

      • getCommissionRate

        public TField getCommissionRate()
        Returns the CommissionRate

        Field description: This is the default rate per annum to be charged on the principal amount of the contract. This rate will be used to calculate the fiduciary commission when no rate has been entered on the fiduciary contract. Input at this field must be within the maximum and minimum allowed for a fiduciary contract as specified on the LMM.INSTALL.CONDS table at the fields called Fid.Max.Comm.Rate and Fid.Min.Comm.Rate. Validation Rules: Standard rate format 6,9. (Multivalue field associated with field 4 Amount.Limit) (Mandatory Input.)
