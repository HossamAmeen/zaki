      • addSelection

        public void addSelection(TField param)
        Appends the specified Selection to the end of the collection.
        Throws an UnsupportedOperationException if the object is not modifiable.

        Field description: Fields SELECTION to ADD.COF.DEPT form a related multi-value set and used as pool funding parameters. The details are used to default the refinancing as well as additional cost of funds parameters in the records of BALANCE.MOVEMENT (BM) file. While BM records are created for an Account or Contract, the first matching Selection condition would be identified for defaulting values. If the Account or contract does not match any of the defined conditions, then no defaulting (explained below) would take place.. In case of accounts and contracts (in LD, MM and MG Applications) if matched refinancing rates are not defined for an individual account or contract, then values defined for refinancing (MI.CR.INT.KEY to FUNDING.DEPT) in the matched Selection, would be defaulted in their BM records. Further, if both refinancing rates and additional cost of funds details are not defined for an individual account or contract (in LD, MM and MG Applications), in addition to the refinancing parameters (as explained above), the parameters for additional cost of funds (ADD.COF.TYPE to ADD.COF.DEPT) in the matched Selection, would be also defaulted in their BM records. For other applications, if BM records are created, the parameters defined in the matched Selection, would be used to default both the refinancing and additional cost of funds values in their BM records. Validation Rules: Must be a valid record on the MI.SELECTION file. Optional Input.

        Parameters:
        param - the Selection to append
        Returns:
        nothing

      • setSelection

        public void setSelection(java.lang.CharSequence param, int idx)
        Set the specified Selection to a specific position in collection.
        Throws an UnsupportedOperationException if the object is not modifiable.

        Field description: Fields SELECTION to ADD.COF.DEPT form a related multi-value set and used as pool funding parameters. The details are used to default the refinancing as well as additional cost of funds parameters in the records of BALANCE.MOVEMENT (BM) file. While BM records are created for an Account or Contract, the first matching Selection condition would be identified for defaulting values. If the Account or contract does not match any of the defined conditions, then no defaulting (explained below) would take place.. In case of accounts and contracts (in LD, MM and MG Applications) if matched refinancing rates are not defined for an individual account or contract, then values defined for refinancing (MI.CR.INT.KEY to FUNDING.DEPT) in the matched Selection, would be defaulted in their BM records. Further, if both refinancing rates and additional cost of funds details are not defined for an individual account or contract (in LD, MM and MG Applications), in addition to the refinancing parameters (as explained above), the parameters for additional cost of funds (ADD.COF.TYPE to ADD.COF.DEPT) in the matched Selection, would be also defaulted in their BM records. For other applications, if BM records are created, the parameters defined in the matched Selection, would be used to default both the refinancing and additional cost of funds values in their BM records. Validation Rules: Must be a valid record on the MI.SELECTION file. Optional Input.

        Parameters:
        param - the Selection to append
        idx - the position in collection
        Returns:
        nothing
