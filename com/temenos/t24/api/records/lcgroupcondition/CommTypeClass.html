      • getCommPercent

        public TField getCommPercent()
        Returns the CommPercent

        Field description: Fields COMM.TYPE to COMM.F.AMT form a related multi value set. Specifies the rate used to calculate the commission amount as a percentage of the standard commission amount. Where a customer or group of customers are given preferential commission rates this field is used to define what percentage of the standard commission is applied. Where no commission is to be charged, a rate of 0% should be input. T24 will compare the calculated commission amount with the OVERALL.MIN.AMT and the OVERALL.MAX.AMT defined in the FT.COMMISSION.TYPE record. If the calculated commission amount is outside of this range then the minimum or maximum amounts specified in the commission table will be applied regardless. This is to ensure the bank always covers its costs and doesn't charge excessive commissions. Validation Rules: Up to 10 type R characters plus a decimal point (standard rate format), but must not exceed 100%. Rate input must be in the range 0-100% Not more than 3 integers may be entered. Not more than 9 decimals may be entered. Mandatory if COMM.TYPE is 'ALL'. Mutually exclusive with COMM.CCY.

        Parameters:
        Returns:

        public TField getCommType()

        Field description: Fields COMM.TYPE to COMM.F.AMT form a related multi value set. Defines the commission type for which a customer or group of customers will be given special conditions.
