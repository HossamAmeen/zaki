      • getCurrNo

        public String getCurrNo()
        Returns the CurrNo

        Field description: Local Tax rate to be deducted from all interest payments made on Bonds held. Where local is the specified Local Government levy. The system allows for the deduction of two sorts of tax SOURCE AND LOCAL. Where the source tax is levied according to the rules and regulations as laid down by the Country of Domicile of the security concerned. The Local tax is deducted according to the domicile of the Institution holding the safecustody account and carrying out the payment on the client concerned. Validation Rules: Up to 5 integers allowed.
