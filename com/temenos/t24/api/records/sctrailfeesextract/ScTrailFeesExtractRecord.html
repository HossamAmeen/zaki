        public TField getBvTotAccrAmtCalcCcy()
        Returns the BvTotAccrAmtCalcCcy

        Field description: Specifies the total recalculated accrual amount in calculation currency that is accrued till date. EXAMPLE:- (The Back valuation process is Triggered After the Creation of the Payment record) :- CALC DATE | EXTR.ACC.AMT 10-JAN-2008 | 100 15-JAN-2008 | 100 16-JAN-2008 | 100 TOT.ACCR.AMT.CALC.CCY = 300 If the SECURITY price is changed on 15-JAN-2008 then the Back valuation process is triggered and Recalculation is done for the 15-JAN-2008, 16-JAN-2008 and it will Update in the BV.EXTR.ACC.AMT with the new recalculated values. The BV.CAL.DATE is updated with the value as 15-JAN-2008 and 16-JAN-2008. The sum of Recalculated price is stored in the BV.TOT.ACCR.AMT.CALC.CCY After Back valuation service:- CALC DATE |EXTR.ACC.AMT| BV.CALC.DATE | BV.EXTR.ACC.AMT 10-JAN-2008 | 100 | | 15-JAN-2008 | 100 | 15-JAN-2008 | 110 16-JAN-2008 | 100 | 16-JAN-2008 | 100 BV.TOT.ACCR.AMT.CALC.CCY = 310
