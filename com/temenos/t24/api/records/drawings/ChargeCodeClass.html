      • getAmortCharges

        public TField getAmortCharges()
        Returns the AmortCharges

        Field description: This field defines whether charges are to be amortised over the life of the L/C. If this field is set to Yes, then this charge will not be taken into the profit and loss account defined for this charge/commission code immediately, but will be amortised over the life of the L/C contract (From issue date to Expiry date). The charge will only be amortised if CHARGE.ACCOUNT field has been entered. If the Maturity date or the Value date of the drawing is to be changed then, the field RECALCULATE.AMORT in LC.PARAMETERS has to be set to YES so that it recalculates the Amortisation amount according to the Value date/New Maturity Date. However, when the Drawings are discounted using the DR.DISC.AMENDMENTS application, the Amortisation is carried out only as per the original input maturity period in the Drawings. So if a Drawing is Discounted with Foreclosure or Online maturity, the entries of LCAMORT account move into P&L account according to the original maturity date of the Drawings and not on the new maturity date or online. Validation Rules: 'Y' or 'N'. Associated Multi-value field. Default from FT.CHARGE.TYPE or FT.COMMISSION.TYPE using charge code. If the charge is being taken immediately (see CHARGE.STATUS) , then this field must be set to 'N'.

      • getChargeAccount

        public TField getChargeAccount()
        Returns the ChargeAccount

        Field description: This field identifies the account where charges are to be debited. Can be any account including internal account and or nostro. If the account is equal to DRAWDOWN.ACCOUNT then the reimburse amount will be added by the charge. CHARGE.STATUS allowed be 8 or 12, depending on whether it is a sight drawing (SP) or acceptance drawing (AC/DP) If the account is equal to PAYMENT.ACCOUNT, then the payment amount will be deducted by that amount. CHARGE.STATUS allowed will be 9 or 11, depending on whether it is a sight drawing (SP), or an acceptance drawing (AC/DP) If the account is any other account than the two above, CHARGE.STATUS can be null or 2. If PARTY.CHARGED were to be CO or CB, this field would be defaulted with DRAWDOWN.ACCOUNT or PAYMENT.ACCOUNT respectively. However in this case, say an Export payment of GBP 100,000 is to be realized from a GBP Nostro to into a Customer's account in Local currency (other than GBP, say USD) and realization happens for GBP 99,200.00, the PARTY.CHARGED code would be used as CB and the account defaulted would be Customer's USD account. But if GBP undergoes a rate change prior to maturity of this Drawing, the settlement amount may not happen for GBP 99,800 but for a small +/- amount depending on the movement of the rate, creating an inconsistency in the Nostro account. In order to eliminate this, when the PARTY.CHARGED is either CO or CB, the User can manually override the Defaulted account, with that of other, i.e., for CO with PAYMENT.ACCOUNT and for CB with DRAWDOWN.ACCOUNT. For further details see help text under field PARTY.CHARGED.

      • getChargeStatus

        public TField getChargeStatus()
        Returns the ChargeStatus

        Field description: Code to identify the current status of the charge. Used to identify if the charge has been taken, claimed,waived or not taken. The system will update this field whenever the charge is processed and no operator input will be allowed. The codes that are used to identify various status of the charge are as follows : blank - The charge has not been taken yet 1 - Charge will be taken in drawing but drawing is not authorised 2 - The charge has been taken 3 - The charge has been claimed 4 - A claimed charge has been settled 7 - Charge Unpaid Reversed 8 - Charge has been taken by adding the amount to a reimbursement claim via a drawing. 9 - Charge has been taken by deducting the amount from a payment via a drawing. 11 - Charge will be taken by deducting the amount from a payment (status 9) at maturity 12 - Charge will be taken by adding the amount to a reimbursement claim (status 8) at maturity 15 - The charge has been waived. Notes : (i) For status 3, the charge is made by debiting the CRF Claimed bucket and crediting the profit and loss account for the charge (found on the FT.CHARGE.TYPE or FT.COMMISSION.TYPE tables). A claim advice is sent to the charge party. When the charge is paid the operator will manually access the LC.ACCOUNT.BALANCES file, where all the charge details are recorded after authorization of the L/C, update the file to indicate that the payment has been received, and the CRF Claimed bucket reimbursed. (ii) When a drawing is entered whereby payments are made or reimbursement is required, then the charge amount will be either deducted from the payment amount or added to the reimbursement amount in order to make a profit and loss entry. The charge status that was initially blank will change as applicable to charge status 8 or 9. (iii) When the charge status is 15, the related charge is considered waived and written in LC.ACCOUNT.BALANCES. This charge cannot be collected in the future and the related tax will not be stored in LC.ACCOUNT.BALANCES. Validation Rules: 1 or 2 - digit number. Associated multivalue field.

        Returns:
        TField - The ChargeStatus

      • getPartyCharged

        public TField getPartyCharged()
        Returns the PartyCharged

        Field description: This field displays a code used to identify from whom the charge is to be taken. For all charges within the multivalue set, this can be different. Some of the charges can be levied against the Opener, others against the Beneficiary, and some against the Third Party. SIGHT DRAWINGS (SP) - If party charges is O, CHARGE.STATUS will be defaulted to 8 if no charge account is input or if the charge account is equal to REIMBURSE.ACCOUNT. - If party charged is B, CHARGE.STATUS will be defaulted to 9, if no charge account is input or if the charge account is equal to PAYMENT.ACCOUNT. - If party charged is CO, only reimburse account is defaulted as charge account, with an option for User to override with that of Payment account and the status should be 10 only. - If party charged is CB, only payment account is defaulted as charge account, with an option for User to override with that of Reimburse account and the status should be 10 only. ACCEPTANCE, DEFERRED PAYMENT (AC/DP) DRAWINGS - If party charged is O, CHARGE.STATUS will be defaulted to 12, if no charge account is input or if the charge account is equal to REIMBURSE.ACCOUNT - If party charged is B, CHARGE.STATUS will be defaulted to 11, if no charge account is input or if the charge account is equal to PAYMENT.ACCOUNT. - If party charged is CO, only reimburse account is defaulted as charge account, with an option for User to override with that of the Payment account and and the status should be 13 only. - If party charged is CB, only payment account is defaulted as charge account, with an option for User to override with that of the Reimburse account and the status should be only 13 only. If the charge account input is an account other than reimburse or pay account, then PARTY.CHARGED WILL BE NULL. and the operator has to input 2 or leave it at null status, depending on the requirement whether to collect the charges now or later. NOTE: This signifies the Discount Rate applicable on the Contract for the period mentioned in this associated multi-value set. If party charged is (CO), it will be understood as Debit plus charges and pay plus charges. If party charged is CB, it will be understood as Debit less charges and pay less charges. If party charged is (T), Then the status can either be null or 2. The third party account in LC needs to be set, if party charged T is to be invoked in DRAWINGS. Validation Rules: (O)pener, (T)hird Party, (B)eneficiary, (CO)rrespondent charges to the opener or (CB)-Correspondent charges to the beneficiary. Associated multi-value field. Default from CHARGES.FROM field above. If 'T' is specified, then THIRD.PARTY.CUSTNO must be set.

        Returns:

      • getRelatedDrawing

        public TField getRelatedDrawing()
        Returns the RelatedDrawing

        Field description: System maintained field. Contains the last two digits of the drawing ID (LCNUMBER) to uniquely identify the charge for this drawing. If the charge has been taken by deduction from the payment amount or addition to the reimbursement amount, then the digits here will be appended by a '-' and the last two digits of the drawing ID. When charges are input or defaulted for a new drawing, the value here will initially contain the last two digits of the Drawing ID. If during validation, the charge entries are made by deducting the charge amount from the payment amount, or by adding the charge amount to the reimbursement amount, then the drawing number here will be appended by a '-' which will indicate that the charge has been taken by this drawing. If any charges are outstanding on the LC.ACCOUNT.BALANCES file, then these will also be taken if a payment or a reimbursement made on this drawing, and the CHRG.REL.DRAW will indicate the above mentioned two digits and '-'. Validation Rules: System maintained field. 5 type A (Alphanumeric) characters. Associated multivalue field his is a NOINPUT field.

        public void setChargeStatus(java.lang.CharSequence value)
        Set the specified value for ChargeStatus
        Throws an UnsupportedOperationException if the object is not modifiable.

        Field description: Code to identify the current status of the charge. Used to identify if the charge has been taken, claimed,waived or not taken. The system will update this field whenever the charge is processed and no operator input will be allowed. The codes that are used to identify various status of the charge are as follows : blank - The charge has not been taken yet 1 - Charge will be taken in drawing but drawing is not authorised 2 - The charge has been taken 3 - The charge has been claimed 4 - A claimed charge has been settled 7 - Charge Unpaid Reversed 8 - Charge has been taken by adding the amount to a reimbursement claim via a drawing. 9 - Charge has been taken by deducting the amount from a payment via a drawing. 11 - Charge will be taken by deducting the amount from a payment (status 9) at maturity 12 - Charge will be taken by adding the amount to a reimbursement claim (status 8) at maturity 15 - The charge has been waived. Notes : (i) For status 3, the charge is made by debiting the CRF Claimed bucket and crediting the profit and loss account for the charge (found on the FT.CHARGE.TYPE or FT.COMMISSION.TYPE tables). A claim advice is sent to the charge party. When the charge is paid the operator will manually access the LC.ACCOUNT.BALANCES file, where all the charge details are recorded after authorization of the L/C, update the file to indicate that the payment has been received, and the CRF Claimed bucket reimbursed. (ii) When a drawing is entered whereby payments are made or reimbursement is required, then the charge amount will be either deducted from the payment amount or added to the reimbursement amount in order to make a profit and loss entry. The charge status that was initially blank will change as applicable to charge status 8 or 9. (iii) When the charge status is 15, the related charge is considered waived and written in LC.ACCOUNT.BALANCES. This charge cannot be collected in the future and the related tax will not be stored in LC.ACCOUNT.BALANCES. Validation Rules: 1 or 2 - digit number. Associated multivalue field.

        Parameters:
        value - The value to set
        Returns:
        nothing

        Field description: This field displays a code used to identify from whom the charge is to be taken. For all charges within the multivalue set, this can be different. Some of the charges can be levied against the Opener, others against the Beneficiary, and some against the Third Party. If the charge account input is an account other than reimburse or pay account, then PARTY.CHARGED WILL BE NULL. and the operator has to input 2 or leave it at null status, depending on the requirement whether to collect the charges now or later. If party charged is (T), Then the status can either be null or 2. The third party account in LC needs to be set, if party charged T is to be invoked in DRAWINGS. Validation Rules: (O)pener, (T)hird Party, (B)eneficiary, (CO)rrespondent charges to the opener or (CB)-Correspondent charges to the beneficiary. Associated multi-value field.
