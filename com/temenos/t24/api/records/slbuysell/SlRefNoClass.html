      • getConsideration

        public TField getConsideration()
        Returns the Consideration

        Field description: Indicates the amount of consideration for buy/sell of the loan. A loan may be traded at premium or discount. The consideration amount includes the premium or discount. For a Non-Contingent buy/sell involving GLOBUS Bank, fund flow would take place for the value in this field. For a Contingent buy/sell involving GLOBUS Bank, fund flow would take place for the discount or premium amount only. The consideration may be expressed either as an absolute amount or as a percentage to the value under consideration. If expressed in absolute amount, the same may be input in this field. Example 1) GLOBUS Bank sells its share of USD 100,000 in a loan to Participant A. The loan is sold for a consideration of USD 110,000 (at a premium). In such case either the consideration amount of USD 110,000 may be entered in this field or the consideration percentage of 110% may be entered in Cons.Perc field. In this case GLOBUS Bank would receive USD 110,000 from Participant A of which USD 10,000 would be accounted as profit on sale of loan. Validation Rules: Input allowed only for Txn.Type 'BS'. Input allowed either in this field or in Cons.Perc field.

        Returns:
