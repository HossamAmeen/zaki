        public TField getAmountFcy()
        Returns the AmountFcy

        Field description: This field is a copy of CATEG.ENTRY -> AMOUNT.FCY Field. Field contains the Foreign Currency Amount from CATEG.ENTRY file. If the Profit or Loss was earned on a foreign Currency Account, or a foreign currency transaction this field contains the Amount of the Entry in the Currency of the originating Account or transaction. Not used in Entries where the origniating Account or transaction was in local Currency.

        Validation Rules: 1. 1-14 numeric characters plus a decimal point (standard amount format, type AMT) signed, or null. Internal file. 2. This is a NOINPUT field.
