      • getRatesEffectiveDate

        public TField getRatesEffectiveDate()
        Returns the RatesEffectiveDate

        Field description: Specifies a future date on which the rates defined in the associated new margin rate fields will take effect andthe rates defined in the current margin rate fields will be discarded. Thus at the end of day of the effective date, the new margin rates will replace the current margin rates definedin the record and this effective date and the associated new margin rates will be deleted. The current margin rates will continue to apply on existing and new contracts until the new rates becomeeffective on the effective date. This field is optional and if new margin rates are defined without any effective date, the rates will take effectimmediately. Validation Rules: Standard T24 Date Field. Only future dates can be defined. Duplicate values are not allowed. The rates that are to take effect immediately (new margin rates defined without effective date) must be definedas the first multivalue set.

        Parameters:
        Returns:
