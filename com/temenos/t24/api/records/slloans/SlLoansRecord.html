        Field description: Identifies the manner in which overdue payments are handled. The default for all contracts is AUTOMATIC. INTERNAL - In this scenario the Agent would pay the participants only on actual receipt of funds in his Nostro/liquidation account. This front-end could also be used in case the Agent does a skimming of the interest (on loans) or commitment fee. In this case the borrower is charged a different rate than the one quoted by the participants and the Agent skims the difference.
