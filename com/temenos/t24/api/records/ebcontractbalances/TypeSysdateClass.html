      • getTypeSysdate

        public TField getTypeSysdate()
        Returns the TypeSysdate

        Field description: The information held here is in the following format: BALANCE.TYPE - [SYSTEM.DATE] The BALANCE.TYPE is the Asset type of the underlying entry to that contract. It may be related to the Principal balances or amount fields such as interest reserves, accrued charges, commissions, etc. BALANCE.TYPE will only be populated if supplied on the underlying entry. SYSTEM.DATE is the date on which the entry for that balance type has been raised. Note: The first transaction posted to an Account will update the Balance.Type 'NILOPEN'. The COB will then move it under the correct Balance.Type according to the sign of the Account(DEBIT or CREDIT). Each time the EB.CONTRACT.BALANCES is updated, the balances and movements are merged under the corresponding Balance.Type leaving out the dates(for the dates prior to the last working day). Validation Rules: A valid hard-coded Balance.Type or a composite of Balance.Type-System.date Multi-value field associated with Value.Date Internal file, no input. .

        Parameters:
        Returns:
