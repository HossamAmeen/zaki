        public TField getCr2CalculType()
        Returns the Cr2CalculType

        Field description: Determines whether the calculation will be performed up to the credit balance Level or in Bands where the interest calculation employed is dependent upon the size of credit balance. (CR2 fields specify a second interest which may be paid.) If more than one set of fields CR2.BASIC.RATE to CR2.LIMIT.AMT are input, this indicates that a different interest rate is applicable depending on the size of the credit balance. CR2 CALCUL TYPE specifies whether separate calculations are to be made for each Band of the credit balance or one is to be made on the entire balance, dependent on the Level of the balance. Example : Assume a credit balance of 15,000 and a Group Credit Interest record which specifies an applicable rate of 10 per cent up to 10,000 and 15 per cent above 10,000. If Band is specified, 2 calculations will be performed, i.e.: 10 per cent on 10,000 15 per cent on 5,000 If Level is specified, 1 calculation will be performed, i.e. 15 per cent on 15,000. Validation Rules: 'BAND' or 'LEVEL' or any abbreviation thereof. (Mandatory input if more than one set of fields CR2.BASIC.RATE to CR2.LIMIT.AMT are specified, otherwise optional. Not allowed if field INTEREST.DAY.BASIS contains 'NONE'.)
