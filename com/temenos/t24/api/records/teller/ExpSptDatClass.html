        public TField getExpSptAmt()
        Returns the ExpSptAmt

        Field description: TELLER EXP. SPT. AMT This field will be updated with the relative amount based on the exposure conditions. This is an associated multi-value field and depending on the exposure ladder this field will be multi-valued. To illustrate If the T24 date is 10.12.2003 and the exposure ladder is defined as 02Days 20% and 04Days 30% for an amount of say 100000 USD Then in this case - the exposure ladder will be multi-valued three times and the EXP. SPT. AMT field will be updated with 50000, 20000 and 30000. Validation Rules: Field can be input with amount.
