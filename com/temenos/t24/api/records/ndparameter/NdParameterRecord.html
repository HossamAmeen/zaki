        public TField getFttCollCptyTax()
        Returns the FttCollCptyTax

        Field description: Provides the option to T24 bank to collect the Financial Transaction Tax payable by the Counterparty also. If yes, accounting entries are raised to collect counterpartys tax wherein customer Account is debited and Tax suspense Account is credited. The values No or Null means T24 bank does not collect the FTT payable by the counterparty or in other words the counterparty himself remits the FTT. This option is provided on the premise that where the counterparty is a customer of the bank ( not an interbank counterparty) the T24 bank assumes responsibility to collect and pay the FTT payable by the customer Validation Rules: Values allowed are Yes, No or Null. Value present in this field is defaulted to FTT.COLL.CPTY.TAX field on ND.DEAL which can be overridden by the User.
