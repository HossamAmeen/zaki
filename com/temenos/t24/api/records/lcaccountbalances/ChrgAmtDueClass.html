      • getChrgRelDraw

        public TField getChrgRelDraw()
        Returns the ChrgRelDraw

        Field description: Identifies the Letter of Credit or Drawing that this charge relates to. The first two digits in this field will identify either the drawing number if the value is greater than '00' or the Letter of Credit if the value is '00', that this charge relates to. If a '-' and a further two digits follows this number then this will identify the number of the drawing that has generated the charges by either a deduction from the payment amount, or addition to the reimbursement amount. If this field only contains two numeric digits, then this will indicate the drawing number that this charge relates to (i.e. this charge has been generated due to the input/change of this drawing). If the drawing number is appended with a '-' and another drawing number, then this will indicate that the charge was generated by the input/change of the first drawing number, but the entries have been produced for this charge by the second drawing number, by the deduction of the charge(s) from the payment amount, or the addition of the charge(s) to the reimbursement amount. For example, if a letter of credit is input and a number of charges are made for the transaction, but are not taken immediately, i.e. CHARGE.STATUS is blank, then a sight payment drawing is entered under this L/C which will generate payment and reimbursement entries. When a payment has to be made, all outstanding charges/commissions, at that time, will automatically be paid by deducting the relevant amount(s) from the payment to be effected or added to the reimbursement amount and credited to the charge P/L account. So the purpose of the second number in this field is to identify which drawing has generated the entries for the outstanding charge(s). In order to identify how the charge was paid see the CHARGE.STATUS field below. Validation Rules: 5 type A (Alphanumeric) characters. System Generated. Associated multivalue field associated with fields CHRG.AMT.DUE through to DATE.RECEIVED. The field will be calculated by the system and no operator input will be allowed.

      • getChrgStatus

        public TField getChrgStatus()
        Returns the ChrgStatus

        Field description: Code to identify the current status of the charge. Used to identify if the charge has been taken, claimed, or not taken or waived. The system will update this field whenever the charge is processed and no operator input will be allowed. The codes that are used to identify the various states of the charge are as follows: blank - The charge has not been taken yet 2 - The charge has been taken 3 - The charge has been claimed 4 - A claimed charge has been settled 7 - Charge Unpaid Reversed 8 - Charge has been taken by adding the amount to a reimbursement claim via a drawing. 9 - Charge has been taken by deducting the amount from a payment via a drawing. 11 - Charge will be taken by deducting the amount from a payment (status 9) at maturity 12 - Charge will be taken by adding the amount to a reimbursement claim (status 8) at maturity

        14 - Charge will be refunded 15 - Charge has been waived. Notes: (i) For status 3, the charge is made by debiting the CRF Claimed bucket and crediting the profit and loss account for the charge (found on the FT.CHARGE.TYPE or FT.COMMISSION.TYPE tables). A claim advice is sent to the charge party. When the charge is paid, the operator will manually access the LC.ACCOUNT.BALANCES file, where all the charge details are recorded after authorization of the L/C, update the file to indicate that the payment has been received, and the CRF Claimed bucket reimbursed. (ii) When a drawing is entered whereby payments are made or reimbursement is required, then the charge/commission amount will be either deducted from the payment amount or added to the reimbursement amount in order to make a profit and loss entry. The charge status that was initially blank will change as applicable to change status 8 or 9. (iii) When the charge status is 15,the related charge is considered waived. This charge cannot be collected in the future and the related tax will be stored in LC.ACCOUNT.BALANCES. Validation Rules:

        1 or 2 Digit code

        Associated multi value field

        The status can only be changed from charge status 3 (claimed charge) to status 4 (settled claim) whereby the SETTLE.AC.FROM field must be entered to specify which account to debit for the charge.

        Status '14' is allowed with "2" or "8" or "9" can be refunded.
