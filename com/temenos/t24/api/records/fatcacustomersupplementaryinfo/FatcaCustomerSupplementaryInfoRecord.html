        Field description:

        The field is used to record the Citizenship details of the customer. This field is multi-valued to enable the capture of dual citizenship.

        If the Nationality or Citizenship of the customer is US, the customer will be considered a potential US customer with STRONG Indicia

        Validation rules:

        2-9 type SSS (uppercase alpha) characters.

        The code will be the ID of an existing record in the COUNTRY table.
