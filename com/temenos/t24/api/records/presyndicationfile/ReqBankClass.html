        public TField getReqBank()

        Field description: Fields Req Bank to Comments are used to record details of circulation of information memorandum. The details of banks name to whom the memo was circulated, their responses and comments are recorded in this set of fields. In Req.Bank field, the name of the bank to whom the memo was sent is input.
