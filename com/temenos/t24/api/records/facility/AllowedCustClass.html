        public TField getAllowedAmt()
        Returns the AllowedAmt

        Field description: The maximum amount that the Allowed.Cust could draw under the facility. When a facility is extended to a Conglomerate, there could be more than one customer who is eligible to draw under the facility. If the user desires to restrict the amounts drawn by a specific customer, the maximum exposure level to this client could be defined in this field. Any drawing resulting in exposure (aggregate live drawings including the drawing that is input) breaching this level would not be allowed. Validation Rules: 1-14 numeric characters plus a decimal point Maximum allowed value is the SL.AMOUNT Allow amount for a customer may not exceed the facility amount less aggregate reserved amount for others in the group Negative values not allowed. Aggregate of Allowed.Amt could exceed the SL.AMOUNT

      • getReservedAmt

        public TField getReservedAmt()
        Returns the ReservedAmt

        Field description: The amount of facility that is reserved for the Allowed.Cust The amount that is mentioned in this field is reserved for the allowed customer and not available for others in the group. Ex.; A, B, C are part of a Conglomerate where A is the facility customer. A credit line of 1,000,000 is shared as follows; Customer Reserved.amt Allow.amt A 250,000 400,000 B 100,000 250,000 C 300,000 500,000 Total 650,000 ----- In this case the 'free float' in the facility is 1,000,000 less the aggregate reserved amount of 650,000, ie, 350,000. This 'free float' is usable on a first come basis subject to the allow amount not getting breached. In order to protect the reserved amount for others in the group, the allowed amount for a specific customer may not exceed the facility amount less the aggregate of reserved amount of all other customers. In this specific example, the allowed amount for A may not exceed 600,000 (1,000,000 less 400,000 reserved for B and C). Both allowed and reserved amounts are expressed in facility currency (Sl.Currency) Validation Rules: 1-14 numeric characters plus decimal point. Negative values not allowed Aggregate Reserved.Amt may not exceed Sl.Amount Allow amount for a customer may not exceed the facility amount less aggregate reserved amount for others in the group Must be less than or equal to Allowed.Amt for the respective Allow.customer
