      • getOperand

        public TField getOperand()
        Returns the Operand

        Field description: The operand to decide whether ONE or ALL of the multiple conditions stipulated are to be satisfied for the rule to apply.OR - One of the conditions should satisfyALL - All the conditions are to be satisfiedExample (a) - A score of 10 points is given if either ratio ABC is less than 1 or ratio CBA is less than 5. The definition in this case will be as follows;2.1 Operand OR3.1.1 Ratio.Type ABC4.1.1 Decision LT5.1.1 Decision fr 1.006.1.1 Decision to3.1.2 Ratio.Type CBA4.1.2 Decision LT5.1.2 Decision fr 5.006.1.2 Decision to7.1 FS.Score 10Example (b) - A score of 15 is applicable if ratio DEF value is less than 1 and ABC ratio value is less than 2. In this case, the definition is as follows;2.1 Operand AND3.1.1 Ratio.Type DEF4.1.1 Decision LT5.1.1 Decision fr 1.006.1.1 Decision to3.1.2 Ratio.Type ABC4.1.2 Decision LT5.1.2 Decision fr 2.006.1.2 Decision to7.1 FS.Score 15Note that if multiple conditions are satisfied, the scores for each satisfied set will be reckoned and hence care should be exercised in defining the rule.Example (c) - If value of ratio ABC is less than 1 the points are 10, for value 1-1.99 the points are 15, for value 2-2.99 the points are 20 and for value greater than or equal to 3 the points are 25. In this case the definition should be as follows;2.1 Operand OR3.1.1 Ratio.Type ABC4.1.1 Decision LT5.1.1 Decision fr 1.006.1.1 Decision to7.1 FS.Score 102.2 Operand OR3.2.1 Ratio.Type ABC4.2.1 Decision RG5.2.1 Decision fr 1.006.2.1 Decision to 1.997.2 FS.Score 152.3 Operand OR3.3.1 Ratio.Type ABC4.3.1 Decision RG5.3.1 Decision fr 2.006.3.1 Decision to 2.997.3 FS.Score 202.4 Operand OR3.4.1 Ratio.Type ABC4.4.1 Decision GE5.4.1 Decision fr 3.006.4.1 Decision to7.4 FS.Score 25In the above case if the actual score is say 2.75 only one condition is satisfied (set 3) and hence 20 points are given.However, if the definition is as follows for the same rule (this is an incorrect definition and should NOT be made)2.1 Operand OR3.1.1 Ratio.Type ABC4.1.1 Decision LT5.1.1 Decision fr 1.006.1.1 Decision to7.1 FS.Score 102.2 Operand OR3.2.1 Ratio.Type ABC4.2.1 Decision LT5.2.1 Decision fr 2.006.2.1 Decision to7.2 FS.Score 152.3 Operand OR3.3.1 Ratio.Type ABC4.3.1 Decision LT5.3.1 Decision fr 3.006.3.1 Decision to7.3 FS.Score 202.4 Operand OR3.4.1 Ratio.Type ABC4.4.1 Decision GE5.4.1 Decision fr 3.006.4.1 Decision to7.4 FS.Score 25In this case if the value of the calculated ration is say 0.90, conditions 1, 2 and 3 are satisfied and hence 10+15+20 = 45 points will be given. Validation Rules: Only allowed values are AND and OR
