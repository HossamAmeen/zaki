        Field description: Specifies whether the fees or charges are to be amortised. The value in this field indicates whether the fees/charges are to be amortised: (Y)es indicates that the component will be amortised (taken into P+L over the life of the contract).       • getPayReceive

        public TField getPayReceive()
        Returns the PayReceive

        Field description: Specifies whether the Charge defined is to Paid by the bank (PAY) or Received from the customer (RECEIVE). Default value is RECEIVE. No change is allowed once authorised. When a PAYable charge is processed the amount will be credited to FEE.PAY.ACCOUNT on the LD contract when entered as CHRG.CODE on the LD contract, or as a type F schedule. Similary when a RECEIVEable charge is processed the amount will be debited from the CHRG.LIQ.ACCOUNT on the LD contract. When the charge is linked to P or I schedules, the PRIN.LIQ.ACCT or INT.LIQ.ACCT respectively will be debited or credited.
