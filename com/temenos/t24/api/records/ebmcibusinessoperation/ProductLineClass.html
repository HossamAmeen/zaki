        public TField getActivity()
        Returns the Activity

        Field description: An Activity is a business function which can be triggered by a user, the close of business or by another application like Funds Transfer or Teller. An Activity is property specific named instances of AA.ACTIVITY.CLASS Arrangement property conditions are created and maintained by performing a number of activities on the arrangement during its lifetime, Activities can be performed on user request or automatically according to a schedule.         public TField getProductGroup()

        Field description: Product group defines a higher level of product definition which groups the classes and its properties whilst also deciding on the mandatory properties of the group.       • getProductLine

        public TField getProductLine()
        Returns the ProductLine

        Field description: This would be an AA Product Line. The Product Line provides a high level definition of the business components (Property Classes) that may be required to construct a product belonging to that line. For example the LENDING Product Line will enable users to design and service term loan products such as: Loans (personal, small business, etc.), Mortgages, Lines of Credit etc. The Product Lines are defined by Temenos and cannot be created by the User. A Product Line is described by the Property Classes which constitute it. This is part of an Associated multi-value set (associated with Product Group, Product) ending with the attribute ACTIVITY. Where the intended Business Operation is an AA Activity, then one of these three – Product Line, Product Group or Product must be specified. For those Activity Classes that are not tied to a specific property class (those that are at the ARRANGEMENT level), the definition could well be at this level. This is also applicable for certain static properties like OFFICERS where, the same single Property is re-used across all Product Groups of a Product Line (such as LENDING-UPDATE-OFFICERS Activity).

        Parameters:
        Returns:
        TField - The ProductLine
