      • getSplitChrgEntries

        public TField getSplitChrgEntries()
        Returns the SplitChrgEntries

        Field description: In Teller Transaction, if charges and taxes are defined, normally, the charge and tax amount will be deducted from the related credit or debit transaction of the customer and accounting entries will be raised for the net amount only. It is also possible to split the entries, as one for full transaction amount and the others for the charge, tax related amounts. This field is used to split the charges and tax amounts as separate entries, from the credit or debit transaction amount of the account to which the charges and tax are attached. If 'YES' is defined in this field, the system will generate separate accounting entries for the charges and taxes attached to the credit or debit transaction, instead of netting them. If 'YES' is not defined and left blank, the charge/ tax amount will be adjusted with the respective debit or credit transaction and accounting entry will be raised for the net amount only. This feature is not available for transactions done through Multi -line Teller. Example: A cash deposit in a customer account of USD10000 has a charge of USD100 and tax of USD10. If SPLIT.CHRG.ENTRIES is defined as YES, the following accounting entries are raised:

        Debit Cash account USD10000
        Credit Customer account USD10000
        Debit Customer account for Charges USD100
        Debit Customer account for tax USD10
        Credit P&L Account for charge USD100
        Credit Internal account for tax USD10
        The debit of USD100 for charges and USD10 for tax can be combined into a single debit as USD110, by using the combine transaction feature of TRANSACTION, as explained below. If SPLIT.CHRG.ENTRIES were left blank, the entries would be raised as below
        Debit Cash account USD10000
        Credit Customer account USD9890
        Credit P&L Account for charge USD100
        Credit Internal account for tax USD10
        Note:

      • setSplitChrgEntries

        public void setSplitChrgEntries(TField value)
        Set the specified value for SplitChrgEntries
        Throws an UnsupportedOperationException if the object is not modifiable.

        Field description: In Teller Transaction, if charges and taxes are defined, normally, the charge and tax amount will be deducted from the related credit or debit transaction of the customer and accounting entries will be raised for the net amount only. It is also possible to split the entries, as one for full transaction amount and the others for the charge, tax related amounts. This field is used to split the charges and tax amounts as separate entries, from the credit or debit transaction amount of the account to which the charges and tax are attached. If 'YES' is defined in this field, the system will generate separate accounting entries for the charges and taxes attached to the credit or debit transaction, instead of netting them. If 'YES' is not defined and left blank, the charge/ tax amount will be adjusted with the respective debit or credit transaction and accounting entry will be raised for the net amount only. This feature is not available for transactions done through Multi -line Teller. Example: A cash deposit in a customer account of USD10000 has a charge of USD100 and tax of USD10. If SPLIT.CHRG.ENTRIES is defined as YES, the following accounting entries are raised:

        Debit Cash account USD10000
        Credit Customer account USD10000
        Debit Customer account for Charges USD100
        Debit Customer account for tax USD10
        Credit P&L Account for charge USD100
        Credit Internal account for tax USD10
        The debit of USD100 for charges and USD10 for tax can be combined into a single debit as USD110, by using the combine transaction feature of TRANSACTION, as explained below. If SPLIT.CHRG.ENTRIES were left blank, the entries would be raised as below
        Debit Cash account USD10000
        Credit Customer account USD9890
        Credit P&L Account for charge USD100
        Credit Internal account for tax USD10
        Note:
        The charges and tax entries that are split from the main transaction, alone, can be combined as a single entry by using the fields COMBINE.PROD.CAT, COMBINE.SYSTEM.ID and COMBINE.TXN by selecting one of the TRANSACTION codes relating to the charges.
        In COMBINE.TXN field, along with other TRANSACTION codes that are to be combined with this selected TRANSACTION, the ID of this selected TRANSACTION code also is to be mentioned. Validation Rules: Optional Field with valid values 'YES' or ' ' (Null) The value defined for this field in TELLER.PARAMETER is defaulted to TELLER.TRANSACTIONS created subsequently.

        Parameters:
        value - The value to set
        Returns:
