        public TField getFFeeAccrAcc()
        Returns the FFeeAccrAcc

        Field description: The account to which fee accruals would be posted. If this accrual has to affect the NAV, then this account needsto be included in ACCOUNT.NOS field of fund investment portfolio. The daily accruals would be posted to thisaccount and on pay date, accruals would be reversed and posted to payable account of the portfolio. The contraentry for accruals would be to the charge account as defined in FT.COMMISSION.TYPE record attached. Valid ACCOUNT record Fields from FUND.FEE.DESC to F.FEE.ACCR.AMT would be associated and multi-valued.
