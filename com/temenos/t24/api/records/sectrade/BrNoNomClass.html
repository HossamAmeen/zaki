        public TField getBrNoNom()
        Returns the BrNoNom

        Field description: Specifies the Nominal Amount (ie the face value) of the Security (Field 1) traded. For each Broker the Total Nominal Amount traded may be split into several Nominal Amounts at different Prices.These will be entered as the Nominal Amount followed by the Price relevant to that Amount. i.e. 54.1.1 = 1st Nominal for Customer.1 55.1.1 = Price relevant to 24.1.1 54.1.2 = 2nd Nominal for Customer.255.1.2 = Price relevant to 24.1.2 etc. Validation Rules: 1-18 numeric characters (including up to 6 decimal places dependant on the currency). Multipliers T,M B may be used. Multivalue field, with associated Subvalue fields. Mandatory input. Cannot be changed when SERVICE.REF is populated.
