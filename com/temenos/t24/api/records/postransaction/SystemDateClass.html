      • getSdRvlAmount1

        public TField getSdRvlAmount1()
        Returns the SdRvlAmount1

        Field description: In the case of FOREX initiated transaction: This is the revaluation currency equivalent of the amount bought in the original FOREX contract that gave rise to the FX.TRANSACTION record. It is the amount in the revaluation currency at the rate applicable when the FOREX contract was entered. This field is updated at authorisation of the FOREX contract and remains unchanged during the lifetime of the contract. In all other cases: This is the revaluation currency equivalent of the amount debited in the original contract that created this POS.TRANSACTION record. It is the amount in the revaluation currency at the rate applicable when the FOREX contract was entered. This field is updated at authorisation of the FOREX contract and remains unchanged during the lifetime of the contract. Validation Rules: No input field automatically updated by the system when the FOREX transaction that gave rise to the FX.TRANSACTION record is authorised. It is a multi-valued field and is associated with SYSTEM.DATE This field will only be updated if the system revalues FOREX contracts against a currency other than the local currency. (i.e. The REVAL.CCY field on FX.PARAMETERS has been populated
