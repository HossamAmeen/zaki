      • getNewAmount

        public TField getNewAmount()
        Returns the NewAmount

        Field description: AZ. SCHEDULES NEW. AMOUNT This field is updated for both AZ deposits and Loan a/cs. When the principal amount is changed - due to draw down/repayment in case of Loans and part redemption/roll over in case of Deposits- this field gets updated. VALUE. DATE is an associated multi-value field which stores the DATE from which the above changes become effective. To illustrate:- Loan sanctioned is 3 million and 2 million is already drawn and the customer makes a draw down of the remaining principal on 15.02.2002 NEW. AMOUNT will be input with 1 million and the associated multi-value field VALUE. DATE will be input with the date 15.02.2002. Validation Rules Change in principal either increase or decrease is a pre-requisite.

        Parameters:
        Returns:
        TField - The NewAmount

        public TField getValueDate()
        Returns the ValueDate

        Field description: Defines the date from which the loan/ deposit account is effective. Any date entered earlier than the business date, is validated against the MAX.BACK.DATE defined under the respective AZ Product parameter. The future date entered here cannot be greater than the Forward Maximum Date defined under DATES. The value is populated from AZ.ACCOUNT. Validation Rules Standard date format; Default is current Business Date. No input field
