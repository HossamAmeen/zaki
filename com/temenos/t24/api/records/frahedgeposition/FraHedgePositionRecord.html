        public TField getRevalProfitLoss()
        Returns the RevalProfitLoss

        Field description: Until the Fixing Day, this field will hold the un-realised profit/loss amount computed during the COB. If the deal remains un-fixed after crossing the Fixing date, the value held by this field is the last un-realized Profit or Loss amount computed on Fixing Date. For deals that have been fixed during the day, the field will hold the realized Profit or Loss amount. The un-realized Profit/Loss amount represented by this field will not be booked if the field CALC.OR.BOOK is set to CALC.
