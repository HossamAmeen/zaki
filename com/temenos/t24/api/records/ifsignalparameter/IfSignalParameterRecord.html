        public TField getFrequency()
        Returns the Frequency

        Field description:

        Overview This indicates the Frequency of the signal. Validation Rules This field can have the below values:

        D - Every working day.

        Dnn - Every nnth working day. The possible frequencies can be D1, D2, D3,..

        M - Last weekend of every month.

        Mnn - Every nnth day of each month or on the previous weekend day. The possible frequencies can be M2, M3,..,M31

        W - Every friday or previous working day.

        Y - Last weekend day of the year.

        Ynn - Last weekend day of the nnth month in the year. The possible frequencies can be Y1, Y2, Y3,..,Y12 Input allowed.
