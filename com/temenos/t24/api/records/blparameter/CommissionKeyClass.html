      • getCalendarBased

        public TField getCalendarBased()
        Returns the CalendarBased

        Field description: This is an optional field. If it is decided that the Commission charged should be a on periodic basis and if we define that the value as NULL, the commission will be charged on a non calendar basis and if it is YES, the commission will be on a Calendar based. Calendar comprises of 4 quarters viz. Jan to Mar, Apr to June, July to Sept, Oct to Dec. For example In April, when a BL Contract is back value dated to March, and it was decided to charge on a periodic basis. If we define that the Commission should be charged on a non calendar Basis, (leaving the Calendar Based field as NULL), the commission will be charged from March to May as a block and June to August as another block. However, if we define the Commission to be charged as Calendar Based, (filling the Calendar Based field as YES), the starting block will be taken as Jan to March, since the value date falls in that block and it will charge for two Blocks, one for Jan to Mar and another for Apr to Jun.

        Returns:
