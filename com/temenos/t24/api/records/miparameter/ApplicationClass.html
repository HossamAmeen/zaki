        Field description: Accepts valid Application, REST and ALL. This is to define application wise SOURCE.FIELD for credit and debit which is used when calculating COST OF FUNDS.       • getSourceFieldCr

        public TField getSourceFieldCr()
        Returns the SourceFieldCr

        Field description: The source field to be used when calculating credit cost of funds or additional costs on credit balances is defined in this field, which must be a field on the BALANCE.MOVEMENT file. The field defined here should be one of the following: CR.AVG.BAL, ADJ.CR.AVG.BAL, POST.CR.AVG.BAL CR.AVG.BAL.MTH, ADJ.CR.AVG.BAL.MTH, POST.CR.AVG.BAL.MTH But may be any field on the file. Refer to the BALANCE.MOVEMENT file for further helptext regarding these fields. The source field is used in the calculation of credit cost of funds as the amount, as follows: CREDIT COST OF FUNDS = AMOUNT * RATE * YEAR.FRACTION where RATE is the refinancing rate on credit balances assinged to the contract in the REFIN.RATE.CR field. The source field is also used in the calculation of additional costs on credit balances as the amount, as follows: ADDITIONAL COST OF FUNDS ON CREDIT BALANCES = AMOUNT * RATE * YEAR.FRACTION where RATE is the rate of additional costs on credit balances assigned to the contract in the ADD.COF.RATE.CR field. In both cases, YEAR.FRACTION is the portion of the year according to the interest basis of the contract. If no interest basis is held on the contract, then the interest basis of the local currency is used. Validation Rules: Must be a valid field on the BALANCE.MOVEMENT file. .

        Parameters:
        Returns:
