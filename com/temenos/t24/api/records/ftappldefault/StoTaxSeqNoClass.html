      • getStoTaxPercent

        public TField getStoTaxPercent()
        Returns the StoTaxPercent

        Field description: Specifies the rate used to calculate the Tax Amount for Standing Order as a percentage of the entry value. This field, together with Tax Number will be used for those countries where some Standing Orders (e.g. those in favour of charity organisations receive a beneficial tax treatment. If for example the Standing Order Amount is equal to GBP 10.00 (as defined in Standing Order file) and the tax benefit indicated in these two fields is equal to 30%, the amount which will be eventually transferred will be 7.00 to the charity organisation which in turn can claim the difference ( 3.00) from local authorities. Validation Rules: Up to 10 type R characters plus a decimal point, (Standard rate format) but must less than 100%. Associated multivalue field. (Mandatory input if Tax Number is input, otherwise not allowed.) Rate input must be less than 100%. Not more than 2 integers may be entered. Not more than 9 decimals may be entered.
