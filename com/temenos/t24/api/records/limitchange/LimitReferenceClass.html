        public TField getLimBandLevel()
        Returns the LimBandLevel

        Field description: Specifies the new value used to determine the percentage amount. This field can contain one of two values. The word BAND in this field indicates that the percentages from more than one band (See MATURITY.PERIOD and PERCENTAGE) will be elected and summed to determine the working percentage. The word LEVEL in this field indicates that a single band will be elected as the working percentage. In both cases the bands used will be resolved on the time left before contract maturity. Validation Rules: The following values will be accepted: BAND or LEVEL. Default: LEVEL. Input in this field will only be retained when the fields MATURITY.PERIOD and PERCENTAGE have been given.

        public TField getPercCalcBasis()
        Returns the PercCalcBasis

        Field description: Determines how the percentage amount will be derived from the MATURITY.PERIOD and MAT.PERIOD.PERC fields. This field can contain one of three values. The keyword PRORATA in this field indicates that the calculation will be pro rata according to the time remaining to maturity or the length of a MATURITY.PERIOD.BAND. For pro rata the calculation will be; % x days to maturity ---------------- 365 with the annual period always in days. The keyword PERIOD indicates that the calculation will be prorata according to the time remaining to maturity or the length of a MATURITY.PERIOD.BAND. The pro rata calculation for the PERIOD setting will be: % x period to maturity ------------------ period where period is set according to the MATURITY.BAND.DEFINITION i.e in Days (D) Weeks (W) Months(M) Years (Y) Where a period is a fraction of a band this will be rounded up. When the word FIXED is indicated the calculation will not be pro rata and is calculated as a simple percentage. When PRORATA or PERIOD is used with LEVEL structure, the relevant percentage derived for the remaining contract period will be applied. When PRORATA or PERIOD is used with a BANDED structure, each percentage band falling within the remaining contract period will be applied for the period of the that band. A PRORATA/PERIOD method can result in a total percentage taken which is in excess of 100% of the transaction value. This can be limited by use of the PERCENTAGE.CAP field. Example: The following percentage structure may be defined: Transactions maturing in up to 6 months are recorded at 5% Transactions maturing in up to 12 months are recorded at 6% Transactions maturing in up to 24 months are recorded at 7% Transactions maturing more than 24 months are recorded at 10% This is entered as: MATURITY.PERIOD 6M 12M 24M R MAT.PERIOD.PERC 5% 6% 7% 10% The risk on a 1 million USD loan with a 36 month initial period would be calculcaled as: At input: LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL = LEVEL PERC.CALC.BASIS = FIXED 10% of the value (36 months remaining falls in the rest period) Amount = 10% * 1,000,000 = 100,000 USD LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL = LEVEL PERC.CALC.BASIS = PRORATA 10% of the value (36 months remaining falls in the rest period) Amount = 10% * 1,000,000 * (3 * 365 / 365) = 300,000 USD LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL = BAND PERC.CALC.BASIS = FIXED 5% of the value (the loan falls in the 0 - 6 month band) Amount = 5% * 1,000,000 = 50,000 USD 6% of the value (the loan falls in the 6 - 12 month band) Amount = 6% * 1,000,000 = 60,000 USD 7% of the value (the loan falls in the 12 -24 month band) Amount = 7% * 1,000,000 = 70,000 USD 10% of the value (the loan falls in the Rest band) Amount = 10% * 1,000,000 = 100,000 USD Total 28% = 280,000 USD LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL = BAND PERC.CALC.BASIS = PRORATA Note the number of days in the bands may vary according to the exact dates invloved. 5% of the value for 6 months (the loan falls in the 0 - Example: The following percentage structure may be defined: Transactions maturing in up to 6 months are recorded at 5% Transactions maturing in up to 12 months are recorded at 6% Transactions maturing in up to 24 months are recorded at 7% Transactions maturing more than 24 months are recorded at 10% This is entered as: MATURITY.PERIOD 6M 12M 24M R MAT.PERIOD.PERC 5% 6% 7% 10% The risk on a 1 million USD loan with a 36 month initial period would be calculcaled as: At input: LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL = LEVEL PERC.CALC.BASIS = FIXED 10% of the value (36 months remaining falls in the rest period) Amount = 10% * 1,000,000 = 100,000 USD LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL = LEVEL PERC.CALC.BASIS = PRORATA 10% of the value (36 months remaining falls in the rest period) Amount = 10% * 1,000,000 * (3 * 365 / 365) = 300,000 USD LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL = BAND PERC.CALC.BASIS = FIXED 5% of the value (the loan falls in the 0 - 6 month band) Amount = 5% * 1,000,000 = 50,000 USD 6% of the value (the loan falls in the 6 - 12 month band) Amount = 6% * 1,000,000 = 60,000 USD 7% of the value (the loan falls in the 12 -24 month band) Amount = 7% * 1,000,000 = 70,000 USD 10% of the value (the loan falls in the Rest band) Amount = 10% * 1,000,000 = 100,000 USD Total 28% = 280,000 USD LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL = BAND PERC.CALC.BASIS = PRORATA Note the number of days in the bands may vary according to the exact dates invloved. 5% of the value for 6 months (the loan falls in the 0 - 6 month band) Amount = 5% * 1,000,000 * (182 / 365) = 24,931.51 USD 6% of the value for 6 months (the loan falls in the 6 - 12 month band) Amount = 6% * 1,000,000 * (183 / 365) = 30,082.19 USD 7% of the value for 12 months (the loan falls in the 12 -24 month band) Amount = 7% * 1,000,000 * (365/365) = 70,000 USD 10% of the value for 12 months (the loan falls in the Rest band) Amount = 10% * 1,000,000 * (365/365) = 100,000 USD Total = 225,013.70 USD With 18 months remaining to maturity: LIMIT.BAND.LEVEL = LEVEL PERC.CALC.BASIS = FIXED 7% of the value (18 months remaining falls in the 12 -
