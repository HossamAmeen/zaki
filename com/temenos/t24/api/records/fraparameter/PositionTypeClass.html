      • getBkUrPft

        public TField getBkUrPft()
        Returns the BkUrPft

        Field description: Indicates if the bank wants to book unrealised profit and recognise them directly as a real income in its Profit and Loss accounts. Many regulatory authorities authorise banks to financially recognise the profit only when its is actually realised. According to the value of this parameter, unrealised profits (due to revaluation of FRA positions) will be completely disregarded or on the contrary directly recognised. Validation Rules: "Yes" or "No". Input in this field is mandatory. This field is associated with field range POSITION.TYPE to PROD.RL.LOSS.
