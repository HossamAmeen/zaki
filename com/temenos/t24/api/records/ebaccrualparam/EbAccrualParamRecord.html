      • getIntmedCalcRound

        public TField getIntmedCalcRound()
        Returns the IntmedCalcRound

        Field description: If intermediate rounding is set then rounding will take place for the last value of each group determined by a change of interest, principal or period, bypassing monthly splits. For example we have a contract for 1m for 6 months at 5% with a change of principal in month 301012006 to 01072006. On 15032006 Principal is increased to 1.5M.01 Accruals will take place for the following groups 01 to 3101 1m at 5% 0102 to 2802 1m at 5% 0103 to 1403 1m at 5% 1503 to 3103 1.5m at 5% 0104 to 3004 1.5m at 5% 0105 to 3105 1.5m at 5% 0106 to 3006 1.5m at 5% Current rounding If the whole period is calculated then all unrounded and rounded amounts are summed.Then the difference is added to the final accrued amount (as at 3006) and rounded again. Intermediate rounding The above rounding calculation will take place for the groups from 0101 to 1403, and then again for the groups from 1503 to 3006. If the daily accrual takes place on the final date of a group then with intermediate rounding the groups going back to the last change of principal, interest or period must be used for rounding, if the split is for change of principal, interest or period but will not take place for a normal month end. If intermediate rounding is not set then rounding will take place using all accrual groups.

        Returns:
        TField - The IntmedCalcRound
