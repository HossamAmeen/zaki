      • getAccount

        public TField getAccount()
        Returns the Account

        Field description: This associated multivalue set of fields is used to store the exchange rate and account used for each of the amount type at the time of first authorisation of LD deal. The purpose of writing these fields is to use the historic exchange rate and account for reversal of deals. At the time of first authorisation, the drawdown & liquidation accounts over which accounting entries have been raised and the relevant exchange rates applied are captured. Should a deal be reversed, the reversal entries would be raised over the respective accounts at the respective exchange rate captured. Validation Rules: System written. No input allowed.
