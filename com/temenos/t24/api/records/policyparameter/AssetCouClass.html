      • getAssetFrom

        public TField getAssetFrom()
        Returns the AssetFrom

        Field description: This field denotes a period of time that defines the start of a maturity band and is normally used in conjunction with the field MAT.END . In practise, the remaining life to maturity (in days) for the bond is calculated using the maturity date on the SECURITY.MASTER file. The result is then applied to the maturity band to determine whether or not the bond falls into this band. This field is used for Portfolio modelling and reporting/enquiry purposes. Example : 16.1.1 MAT.START 0D (greater than/equal to 0 days) 17.1.1 MAT.END 6M (less than 6 months) 16.1.2 MAT.START 6M (greater than/equal to 6 months) 17.1.2 MAT.END 1Y (less than 1 year) 16.1.3 MAT.START 1Y (greater than/equal to 1 year) 17.1.3 MAT.END 3Y (less than 3 years) 16.1.4 MAT.START 3Y (greater than/equal to 3 years) 17.1.4 MAT.END 10Y (less than 10 years) 16.1.5 MAT.START 10Y (greater than/equal to 10 years) 17.1.5 MAT.END (as above i.e. infinity) Validation Rules: Optional Input If the field is not entered portfolio modelling processing will not group bonds by maturity band If this field is populated, bonds will be grouped by maturity bands for modelling and reporting purposes.

        Parameters:
        Returns:
        TField - The AssetFrom
