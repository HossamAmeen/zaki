      • getChargeAmt

        public TField getChargeAmt()
        Returns the ChargeAmt

        Field description: Records the principal balance on the deal at each stage throughout the life of the deal. This field contains the amount of the charges described in the currency defined in the associated multi-value field CHARGE.CURR, that will be applied according to the value in associated multi-value field CHARGE.CODE. This field is divided into sub-values with each sub-value CHARGE.AMT being associated with a corresponding CHARGE.CODE. It is taken directly from the MD.DEAL contract and is removed when the charges have been applied. Validation Rules: 1-14 numeric characters plus commas and decimal point Multi-valued field associated with CHARGE.DATE, CHARGE.CURR and CHARGE.CODE (No input allowed)

        Parameters:
        Returns:
