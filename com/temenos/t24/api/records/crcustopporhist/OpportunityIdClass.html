        public TField getOrigDirection()
        Returns the OrigDirection

        Field description: This field will hold the Original direction of the CR.OPPORTUNITY. The system needs to monitor how many outbound opportunities are generated by the bank, to ensure 'over saturation' does not occur where thecustomer is contacted too many times. However, if an opportunity is initially 'INBOUND', i.e reliant on the customer contacting the bank, if it is changed to 'OUTBOUND' following interest from the customer, theseopportunities should not be included in the overall count - hence over saturation relies on the Original direction, rather than the eventual direction of the opportunity.
