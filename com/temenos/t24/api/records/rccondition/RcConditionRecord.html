        BSNSS - Every Business day

        WEEKLY - Every week or XX number of weeks

        MONTHLY -

        Twice Monthly or

        Monthly every XX months on a specific date

        DEFINED - (Must exist EB.FREQUENCY) For example;

        LHFYR -Last day in this Mid Year

        LQUAT - Last day in this quarter

        LYEAR-Last day of this year

        LMNTH - Last day of this month

        LWEEK - Last day of this week

        Standard T24 frequency
