      • getTrPart

        public TField getTrPart()
        Returns the TrPart

        Field description: Name of the participant in the tranche. Multi-value field to assist definition of several participants under a tranche. When a facility is defined in tranches, the ratio of each participant's share in the facility could different at tranche level. It would also be possible to define facilities where different banks participate in each tranche. Ex. Facility of 1,000,000 is subscribed by A = 100,000, B = 200,000, C = 300,000 and D = 400,000. This share is at the top level and the facility has two tranches 001 for 600,000 and 002 for 400,000. The following types of tranche definitions are possible; (A) Ratio of share being different Tranche ref Bank Share Tranche ref Bank Share 001 A 50,000 002 A 50,000 B 100,000 B 100,000 C 200,000 C 100,000 D 2,50,000 D 150,000 (B) Tranche participants are different Tranche ref Bank Share Tranche ref Bank Share 001 D 400,000 002 A 100,000 B 200,000 C 300,000 TOTAL 6,000,000 4,000,000 Validation Rules: 3-10 alphanumeric customer mnemonic or 1-10 numeric character customer code Must be a valid participant of the facility

        Field description: Share of the participant in the respective tranche When a facility is defined in tranches, the ratio of each participant's share in the facility could different at tranche level. It would also be possible to define facilities where different banks participate in each tranche. Ex. Facility of 1,000,000 is subscribed by A = 100,000, B = 200,000, C = 300,000 and D = 400,000. This share is at the top level and the facility has two tranches 001 for 600,000 and 002 for 400,000. In other words, aggregate value of TR.PART.AMT of each participant across tranches should equal PART.AMOUNT Once the facility authorised, this field cannot be input. After first authorisation this field is system maintained. The result of change in share owing to a transaction in SL.BUY.SELL. will be updated in this field.
