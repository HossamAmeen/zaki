      • getTaxChgSt

        public TField getTaxChgSt()
        Returns the TaxChgSt

        Field description: Code to identify the current status of the tax. Used to identify if the tax has been taken, claimed, or not taken. The system will update this field whenever the charge is processed and no operator input will be allowed. The codes that are used to identify various states of the tax are as follows : blank - The tax has not been taken yet 2 - The tax has been taken 3 - The tax has been claimed 4 - A claimed tax has been settled 7 - Charges Unpaid Reversed 8 - Tax has been taken by adding the amount to a reimbursement claim via a drawing. 9 - Tax has been taken by deducting the amount from a payment via a drawing. Notes : (i) For status 3, the charge is made by debiting the CRF Claimed bucket and crediting the profit and loss account for the charge (found on the FT.CHARGE.TYPE or FT.COMMISSION.TYPE tables). A claim advice is sent to the charge party. When the charge is paid the operator will manually access the LC.ACCOUNT.BALANCES file, where all the charge details are recorded after authorization of the L/C, update the file to indicate that the payment has been received, and the CRF Claimed bucket reimbursed. (ii) When a drawing is entered whereby payments are made or reimbursement is required, then the charge amount will be either deducted from the payment amount or added to the reimbursement amount in order to make a profit and loss entry. The charge status that was initially blank will change as applicable to charge status 8 or 9. Validation Rules: System Generated. 1 Digit number. Associated multivalue field.

        Parameters:
        Returns:
        TField - The TaxChgSt

      • getTaxParty

        public TField getTaxParty()
        Returns the TaxParty

        Field description: Code to identify the party this tax applies to. This field identifies from whom the tax is to be taken. For all tax within the multivalue set, these can be different. Some of the tax can be levied against the Opener, others against the Beneficiary, and some against the Third Party. Validation Rules: 'O' or 'T' or 'B'. Associated multivalue field. Default from CHARGES.FROM field above. If 'T' is specified, then THIRD.PARTY.CUSTNO must be set. If no input is made this field will default to CHARGES.FROM.

      • getTaxRelDrg

        public TField getTaxRelDrg()
        Returns the TaxRelDrg

        Field description: Contains the last two digits of the drawing ID (LCNUMBER) to uniquely identify the tax for this drawing. If the tax has been taken by deduction from the payment amount or addition to the reimbursement amount, then the digits here will be appended by a '-' and the last two digits of the drawing ID. When charges are input or defaulted for a new drawing, the value here will initially contain the last two digits of the Drawing ID. If during validation, the charge entries are made by deducting the charge amount from the payment amount, or by adding the charge amount to the reimbursement amount, then the drawing number here will be appended by a '-' which will indicate that the charge has been taken by this drawing. If any charges are outstanding on the LC.ACCOUNT.BALANCES file, then these will also be taken if a payment or a reimbursement made on this drawing, and the CHRG.REL.DRAW (Field 10.1) will indicate the above mentioned two digits and '-'. Validation Rules: System Generated. 5 type A (Alphanumeric) characters.

        Parameters:
        Returns:
        TField - The TaxRelDrg
