        public TField getJointCustTaxid()
        Returns the JointCustTaxid

        Field description: Specifies the joint holder customer id "*" tax.id appied The combination of joint customer id and tax.id should be a unique occurance under a customer*income.type combination. Joint customer id is taken from CUSTOMER.RELATIONSHIP record For example, lets say A is the primary owner who owns a portfolio jointly with another customer B in ration 70:30. In this case, there would be two sub-values of this field as: - 10.1.1 A*1010.1.2 B*10 Or customers A and B can have more than one tax (tax and link tax) applied, like10.1.1 A*1010.1.2 A*1210.1.3 B*2010.1.4 B*30
