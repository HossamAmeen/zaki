      • getMovementDate

        public TField getMovementDate()
        Returns the MovementDate

        Field description: Holds the date value for the principal movement entered in PRIN.MOVEMENT occurs. The contract principal amount is changeable throughout the life of the contract. Once the value date has been passed the actual PRINCIPAL.AMOUNT field becomes a no-change field. All principal movements are then entered in the PRIN.MOVEMENT field and update the PRINCIPAL.AMOUNT during the end of day for the MOVEMENT.DATE. Any number of principal movements can be entered but with the following constraint: If more than one movement is entered they must be entered in value date order. Validation Rules: Standard T24 date format. Multi-valued field associated with field 15.1 PRIN.MOVEMENT recording the value dates of the movements entered. (Optional Input) The MOVEMENT.DATE cannot be before the VALUE.DATE. The MOVEMENT.DATE cannot be after the MATURITY.DATE. An override is required if the MOVEMENT.DATE is a non-working day.

        Parameters:
        Returns:
        TField - The MovementDate

      • getPrinMovement

        public TField getPrinMovement()
        Returns the PrinMovement

        Field description: This field is used to allow the user to change the deal PRINCIPAL.AMOUNT during the lifetime of the deal. The deal PRINCIPAL.AMOUNT is changeable throughout the life of the deal. However, once the Value Date has been passed then the actual PRINCIPAL.AMOUNT field becomes a "no change" field. All principal movements to be entered after the WE.DATE use this field. Any number of principal movements may be entered but with the constraint that the outstanding principal can never fall below zero, i.e. an asset can never become a liability and vice versa. Each PRIN.MOVEMENT is linked to a MOVEMENT.DATE. The PRINCIPAL.AMOUNT being changed according to the PRIN.MOVEMENT during the day end of the MOVEMENT.DATE. Validation Rules: 1 - 14 numeric characters plus a decimal point, minus sign and comma's. Multi valued field of principal movements associated with field 14.1 MOVEMENT.DATE. Decreases will be prefixed with a minus sign. (Optional Input)

        Parameters:
        Returns:
        TField - The PrinMovement
