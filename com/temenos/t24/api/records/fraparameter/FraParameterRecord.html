        Field description: Provides the option to T24 bank to collect the Financial Transaction Tax payable by the Counterparty also. If yes, accounting entries are raised to collect counterpartys tax wherein customer Account is debited and Tax suspense Account is credited. The values No or Null means T24 bank does not collect the FTT payable by the counterparty or in other words the counterparty himself remits the FTT.
