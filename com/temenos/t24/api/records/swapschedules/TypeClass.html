      • getOrigSchedDate

        public TField getOrigSchedDate()
        Returns the OrigSchedDate

        Field description: Presence of this field is required to handle the processing of various Swap Schedules as per the Day.Convention and Date.Adjustment. It holds the Original date of the schedule. System compares the Orig.Sched.Date with the Process.Date and does the following. a) If the Process.Date is greater than Orig.Sched.Date, then system creates a new Swap.Schedule with ID same as the Process.date, during the COB of the Orig.Sched.Date. Thus the Swap.Schedule is retained for processing at a future date. b)If the Process.Date is less than the Orig.Sched.Date, then system processes the respective Swap.Schedule during the COB of the Process.Date itself. Field Properties Standard T24 date field Maximum of 8 characters.

        Parameters:
        Returns:
        TField - The OrigSchedDate

      • getProcessDate

        public TField getProcessDate()
        Returns the ProcessDate

        Field description: This field determines the date when the schedules will be processed based on the DAY.CONVENTION and the BUSINESS.CENTRES For Example, If DAY.CONVENTION is NULL the process date is the same as the schedule date. "FOLLOWING" the process date will be the first following day that is a business day. "PRECEDING" the process date will be the first preceding day that is a business day. "MODIFIED" the process date will be the first following day that is a business day unless the day falls in the next calendar month, in which case the process date will be the first preceding day that is a business day. Field Properties Standard T24 date field Maximum of 8 characters.

        Parameters:
        Returns:
        TField - The ProcessDate
