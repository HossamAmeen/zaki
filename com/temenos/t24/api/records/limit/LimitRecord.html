        Throws an UnsupportedOperationException if the object is not modifiable.

        Field description: Specifies the next date on which the limit is to be reviewed. The review period is entered in the standard T24 frequency format. The default is for the first review to take place one year from today's date and monthly thereafter up to the time the user updates the limit record with new review and expiry dates. Warning of the approach of this date is given on the Limits Approaching Review Date overnight report. The number of days prior to Review Date that the warning starts to appear is defined by the Expiries Reporting field on the LIMIT.PARAMETER table. The date is then updated by one Frequency. If the Review Date is passed before the Limit has been reviewed and the next Review Date indicated to the System, the Limit will remain available. The Limit does not become unavailable until the Limit Expiry Date (Field 7). Note: If the user wants to include on a daily basis the limits under review in the 'Limits approaching review' report, he can do so by entering the appropriate frequencycode ('D') after the review date. Validation Rules: Standard T24 frequency code. The resulting date of the first review must be later than the APPROVAL.DATE and the OFFERED.UNTIL Date. This rule applies even when Limit Review Date alone is being amended. The first review date must not be later than the Limit EXPIRY.DATE.
