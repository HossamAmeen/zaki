      • getPrinChange

        public TField getPrinChange()
        Returns the PrinChange

        Field description: The amount the principal is to be increased or decreased by on the associated CHANGE.DATE. Principal increases and decreases are allowed for NOTICE and FIXED orders, and must be approved by the dealer before the associated placement is updated with the details. When the principal change is entered and authorised, the associated CHANGE.STATUS will be set to REQUESTED, and the details will be updated on the corresponding FD.FIDUCIARY contract, and the cash flow for the DRAWDOWN.ACCT will be checked. Drawdown of the Principal change for the order is always performed during the end of day. The dealer will accept the request for principal change by entering ACCEPTED in the corresponding CHANGE.STATUS field on the placement contract. Validation Rules: 1-14 amount field, with an optional leading "-" for principal decreases. (Optional input) The sum of the principal plus principal changes must always be greater than zero. The amount of principal change should be a MULTIPLE of the currency of the order defined in FD.FID.CURRENCY. If not an override will be given. The DRAWDOWN.ACCT must be a valid account if a PRIN.CHANGE is present. No null values allowed. Once the associated CHANGE.STATUS is set to ACCEPTED, the associated PRIN.CHANGE and CHANGE date cannot be amended.

        Returns:
