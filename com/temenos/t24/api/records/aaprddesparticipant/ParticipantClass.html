        public TField getParticipantRole()
        Returns the ParticipantRole

        Field description: This field is used to mention the role of the each participant in the loan. The participant can be either a participant or sub-participant. If the role is sub-participant, then the lender is officially not a part of the loan, and the owning bank who has sub-parted their share to this lender is responsible for the obligations towards the borrower.
