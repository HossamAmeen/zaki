      • getLowerTol

        public TField getLowerTol()
        Returns the LowerTol

        Field description: Lower tolerance percentage for PCT.AST.ALOC. Expressed in terms of percentage deviation downwards of value of current holdings compared to required value as per model percentage of total value. Lower tolerance of 10% means that suggestion to buy not generated until current holdings become 10% lower in value than recommended holdings as per model. This field is mandatory if the VALUE.TYPE is 'Percentage' and the ASSET.TYPE is 'Security'. Otherwise must be left blank.

Upper tolerance of 10% means that suggestion to sell not generated until current holdings become 10% higher in value than recommended holdings as per model.
