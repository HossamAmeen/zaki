      • getFieldSelect

        public TField getFieldSelect()
        Returns the FieldSelect

        Field description: This field enables you to define one or more Order Selection Criteria. You may enter any valid field name or number from the SEC.ACC.MASTER (portfolio) application. All tests on a particular field must be made in consecutive entries. In this case, they will be combined in an OR relationship. For example : INVESTMENT.PROGRAM EQ 35 INVESTMENT.PROGRAM EQ 47 when coupled with DEPT.ACCT.OFF EQ 320 will be translated as All SEC.ACC.MASTER accounts with INVESTMENT.PROGRAM EQ 35 OR 47 and with DEPT.ACCT.OFF EQ 320. The relationship between 1 and 2 is therefore OR The relationship between 1 OR 2 and 3 is AND (1 AND 3 and 2 AND 3) Validation Rules: Field must exist on Standard Selection Either Field Number from SEC.ACC.MASTER or Field Name from SEC.ACC.MASTER Standard Selection record Optional Input Must be entered if DEPT.ACCT.OFF not entered

        Parameters:
        Returns:
