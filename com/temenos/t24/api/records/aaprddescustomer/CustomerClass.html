      • getTaxLiabilityPerc

        public TField getTaxLiabilityPerc()
        Returns the TaxLiabilityPerc

        Field description: This field indicates the ownership percentage of the customer in the arrangement with respect to tax liability. Based on the percentage defined for each customer, income will be split and tax will be calculated using the respective tax percentage of each customer and the income split.

        Example: Customer 1 - 50% Customer 2 - 50% Interest capitalization amount: $1000. Tax percentage for customer 1 is 10% and for customer 2 is 0% (since the customer is minor). Now the Tax will be calculated by applying 10% on $500 and 0% on $ 500. So the net tax amount will be $50 for which tax accounting will be raised.

        Associated with CUSTOMER field. Validation rules * Must be equal or less than 100% * Sum of the percentage cannot be more than 100%

        Parameters:
        Returns:
        TField - The TaxLiabilityPerc
