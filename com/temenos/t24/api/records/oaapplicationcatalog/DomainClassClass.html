      • getRole

        public TField getRole()
        Returns the Role

        Field description: A loan application Purpose requires that one or more entities fills-in the application form or simply that information is recorded about one or more entities. All entities filling-in the form (or about whom information is recorded on the form) do not necessarily assume the same role in the application process.Some may play the more prominent role of Main Applicant while some other will be co-applicant , guarrantor etc. The High level group or category of information recorded on the application is referred to in OA as a Domain Class. It can also be understood as information about an entity group. The entity group assumes a distinct ROLE in the application process. The role assumed by the entity group is recorded in the ROLE field and the distinc entities part of this entity group are indicated in the DOMAIN.TYPE field. 1)Associated Multivalue Field beginning with DOMAIN.CLASS, including ROLE , MINIMUM.ROLE, MAXIMUM.ROLE, DOMAIN.TYPE , MIN.DOMAIN.PART , MAX.DOMAIN.PART and ending with FORM 2)Mandatory Input 3) Validation Rules a. Must be a valid record ID in the table AA.CUSTOMER.ROLE
