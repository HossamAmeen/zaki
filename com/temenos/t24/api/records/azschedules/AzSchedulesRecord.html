      • getExceedsTolerance

        public TField getExceedsTolerance()
        Returns the ExceedsTolerance

        Field description: AZ. SCHEDULES EXCEEDS. TOLERANCE This is the field that decides whether to charge late payment fee, levy penalty (on loan arrears) etc. This is decided by the system based on the inputs made in the field LIAB. TO.PENALTY (field in APP) in case of deposits and from GRACE. PERIOD (field in APP) field in case of loans. The parameters for the charges are drawn from AZ. PRODUCT. PARAMETER (APP) and Penalty from AZ. ACCOUNT. TO illustrate: For a savings plan deposit if LIAB. TO .PENALTY is defined as 2- then it will not charge penalty for defaults made upto 2 installments. If a 3rd installment is defaulted -then this field is activated to charge penalty as designed in APP Validation Rules Input in fields LIAB. TO. PENALTY and GRACE. PERIOD is optional. Once input the period is reckoned before calculating penalty. Or else penalty is calculated from DAY ONE.

        Parameters:
        Returns:
        TField - The ExceedsTolerance
