      • getMinMovement

        public TField getMinMovement()
        Returns the MinMovement

        Field description: Defines for commission taken in ARREARS when the minimum and maximum commission amount should be checked while calculating the commission amount. Minimum and Maximum commission is always applied at maturity of an order, but for Notice orders, the minimum and maximum can be applied at only principal or interest movements, or both. If minimum commission is taken only on principal or interest movement, the minimum amount will apply if the total commission taken since the last movement type specified for minimum commission is less than the minimum. e.g: Minimum amount = 100 USD applies at INTEREST movement 31/12/93 - Last interest movement 31/01/94 - Principal movement comm = 20 USD (min not applied) 31/03/94 - Principal movement comm = 30 USD (min not applied) 30/06/94 - Interest movement comm calculated = 40 USD The total commission amount is 90 USD since the previous interest movement, so the minimum applies making the commission actually taken 50 USD. Validation Rules: "PRINCIPAL", "INTEREST" or "BOTH" or any abbreviation thereof. (Optional input.) No input allowed when COMM.TAKEN is ADVANCE. No input allowed for FIXED type, or FIXED category records.

        Parameters:
        Returns:
        TField - The MinMovement

      • getStartEndCalc

        public TField getStartEndCalc()
        Returns the StartEndCalc

        Field description: Specifies whether the commission amount should be calculated as soon as all information required is available, or only at the time commission is taken. When commission is taken in arrears, the user may wish to calculate the commission only when the commission is actually taken, or in advance. Once the commission amount is present on the FD.FID.ORDER record, it is not recalculated by the system. In the case of NOTICE contracts when the START is requested, the amount will only be calculated when the interest settlement, or principal movement date is known. Validation Rules: "START" or "END" or any abbreviation thereof. (Optional input) Input only allowed when COMM.TAKEN is ARREARS.

        Parameters:
        Returns:
        TField - The StartEndCalc
