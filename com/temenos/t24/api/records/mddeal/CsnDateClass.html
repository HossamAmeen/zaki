      • getCsnDate

        public TField getCsnDate()
        Returns the CsnDate

        Field description: When commission has been entered there must be a date or series of dates when the commission will be levied. The validation depends on whether the CSN.PAYMENT.TYPE is entered as BEGIN or END. The commission period is defined by the start of the contract and the end of the contract with optional interim dates specified by the user. If commission is defined then there must be either a schedule for the start date (for BEGIN contracts) or at maturity (for END contracts). Where it is set to END: Accruals are passed to a P/L category according to the cycling frequency and the customer is debited (or credited according to deal type) at the end of the period. For the bank the effect of the P/L is built up over the life of the contract rather than one lump payment at the end. Where it is set to BEGIN: Accruals are passed to a P/L category according to the cycling frequency and the customer is debited (or credited according to deal type) at the start of the period. This amount is then left in an advance P/L account and then dripped through to the real P/L. This gives the same effect of the P/L build up over the life of the contract as the END method. Validation Rules: If the CSN.PAYMENT.TYPE is 'BEGIN' then the first schedule's CSN.DATE must be deal value date If the CSN.PAYMENT.TYPE is 'END' then the LAST schedule's CSN.DATE must be deal maturity date
